Technology

Inside Pulse: Sensors, AI, and Emergency Intelligence.

Pulse’s special technology tracks heart rate, exercise, oxygen levels, and other vitals. It reduces miscommunication, records abnormal signs, and prepares detailed reports that doctors can review—turning everyday wear into a continuous health companion.

Continuous Health Tracking

  • Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV).
  • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO₂) and respiratory trends.
  • Activity, steps, and exercise intensity.
  • Sleep duration and quality estimates.

AI‑Powered Emergency Detection

  • Detects abnormal vitals patterns and potential emergencies.
  • Triggers alerts to the Pulse app, emergency contacts, and EMTs (where enabled).
  • Generates a concise, time‑stamped report for medical professionals.
  • Helps doctors adjust treatment plans based on real‑world data.
Hardware Architecture
PPG Sensor Measures heart rate and blood flow changes using photoplethysmography for continuous monitoring.
Motion Sensors Accelerometer & gyroscope track movement, falls, and activity patterns for context‑aware alerts.
SoC Microcontroller Processes sensor data, runs AI models, and manages Bluetooth connectivity to the Pulse app.
Silicone Interior Soft, skin‑safe silicone ensures secure sensor contact and all‑day comfort.
Steel Exterior Elegant, modern stainless steel shell that looks like jewelry—not a medical device.
PulseTap™

Tap to Share Life‑Saving Information—Securely.

Inspired by how smartphones share contacts when devices are nearby, Pulse introduces PulseTap™—a secure way to share critical medical information with a single tap.

How PulseTap™ Works

  • Press the discreet button on the bracelet to enable PulseTap™ for a short, secure window.
  • Tap the bracelet against a compatible smartphone or EMT device—just like sharing a contact.
  • A medical ID card appears on the screen with essential information.

For bystanders, only limited, non‑sensitive information is shown. For EMTs and authorized personnel, a deeper profile can be unlocked with verification or password.

Access Levels

  • Public View: Name, age, emergency contact, allergies, and critical conditions.
  • EMT View: Full medical history, medications, treatment notes, and physician details.
  • Password Access: Users can share a secure code with trusted caregivers or family.

Pulse facilitates a smoother, faster, and simplified version of emergency medical procedures through continuous health tracking and instant, secure data sharing.
