FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pulse a medical device?

Pulse is a consumer health wearable designed to support wellness and emergency preparedness. It does not replace professional medical equipment.

Does Pulse require a subscription?

No. Pulse works out of the box with basic features. Subscriptions unlock advanced analytics and reports.

How does PulseTap™ work?

EMTs tap the bracelet with their device to instantly access a secure, read‑only medical profile.

Is my data secure?

Your information is encrypted, stored securely, and only accessible to you and authorized first responders.
